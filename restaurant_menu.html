1. Cuy Asado con Papas

A traditional dish of the Andean region consisting of cuy, also known as guinea pig. Served with a side of potatoes and a fresh lettuce and tomato salad, it is considered a delicacy in parts of Bolivia, Peru, and Ecuador.
Price: $20


2. Yaguarlocro

A traditional stew of Ecuadorian cuisine in the Andes with lamb's entrails including brain, intestines, liver, lung, tongue, and rumen. It is usually served with fried lamb blood, avocado and pickled red onion rings.
Price: $15


3. Cariucho de Pollo

A traditional Ecuadorian dish in the Andes composed of baked chicken with an eggy thick sauce and potatoes. It is usually served with a side of salad and some rice.
Price: $18
